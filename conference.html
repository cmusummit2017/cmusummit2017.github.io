Finance Panel

Over the past few years, digital and technological revolution has definitely chanegd the landscape in the financial service industry, creating both challenges and opportunities. Featuring the topic of "FinTech", the 5th CMU Summit finance panel has attracted specialists from leading fintech companies to reveal the mystery of fintech and to disclose world's new initiatives of financial technology. We aim to better incorportae cutting-edge technologies into traditional finance in the era of innovation. We kindly welcome anyone who is interested in this thriving area to join us and contribute to the panel discussion with your innovative idea.

Topics

  • Future of Fintech: Collaboration without boundaries
  • Bridging Finance and Technology:
    • New Opportunities in the Era of Innovative Technology

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Panel

The IE Panel dedicates to create a platform for prestigious investors and entrepreneurs from both China and the US to network, share experience, and exchange ideas. The panel serves to promote bilateral dialogue and cooperation between the two countries. This year, IE Panel will bring up discussions about the industry trend, investment tendency, and commercialization of technology in the leading edge industry, Robotics.

  • Robotics Industry Outlook: Understanding the Current Status and Future Prospects
