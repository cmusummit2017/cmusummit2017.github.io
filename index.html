Who We Are


Founded in 2011, CMU Summit on US-China Innovation and Entreprenuership is a non-profit student organization that aims to connect innovative ideas between US and China. We not only provide an annual conference and new venture competition, but also host online talks every two weeks.


The topics we cover range from Deep Learning to Biomedical Engineering, from Human Computer Interaction to Virtual/Augmented Reality, from gazing into the future technology market to building a successful start-up company. Over the past 6 years, we have attracted more than 5,000 participants and 200 industry leaders. But these are just numbers, what we really care about is to connect you with the most talented and passionate individuals you can ever met right here at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. Come and join us on April 15th, 2017.
